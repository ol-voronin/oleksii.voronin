CASE 01

Unifying engineering UX for an Oil & Gas world leader

Delivery
Lead UX Designer · enterprise well-construction platform · 5 global regions
Concept preview

Integrated technology supporting crucial decision points across the oil and gas life cycle. The challenge: custom solutions for diverse data categories across Saudi Arabia, Norway, Oman, Mexico, and the USA — engineer-centric features, intricate calculations, and regional differences reconciled into one seamless experience.

Process
  • Ran workshop sessions with SMEs and stakeholders; built personas and CJMs to expose gaps and touchpoints across regions.
  • Developed tailored workflows for five engineering roles and a unified information architecture, validated through user testing.
  • Established a "gold" standard: one transparent workflow that absorbs future features without friction.
  • Identified and fixed team-process inefficiencies via a three-phase scrum-of-scrums approach, improving cross-team communication.

CASE 03

Delivery
Senior UX/UI Designer · automation & digitalisation for process manufacturing
Concept preview

Intelligent infrastructure for manufacturing, rail transport, and digital healthcare. The hardest constraint: end users on the factory floor had little digital experience, so every insight had to be earned through research and translated into radically simple design.

Process
  • Deep user interviews and testing with factory workers — 70+ questions per research cycle, personas per user segment.

CASE 04

Interswitch — 12 banking apps for Africa's leading fintech

Delivery
Senior UX/UI Designer · mobile banking · 4 African regions
Concept preview — one template, many brands

Interswitch's payment platform shaped the Nigerian payments landscape and powers its expansion across Africa. My work: designs for 12 bank apps, then a white-label template making every future bank launch dramatically cheaper and faster — while respecting regional cultures, regulatory frameworks, and security.

Process
  • UX audit, stakeholder and user interviews, proto-personas, and a unified workflow for the white-label template.
  • Research-driven information architecture and high-fidelity wireframes, validated with user tests.
  • Started with per-app UI kits, then consolidated into one general design system with internal kits — unifying approaches and cutting development costs.

CASE 05

Accessible cloud data platform for Oil & Gas

Delivery
Senior UX Architect · high-throughput data ingestion · accessibility-first
Concept preview

Cloud software ingesting large volumes of data from diverse sources — modular, plug-and-play, with intelligent workflows. Three user worlds (civil workers, support, engineers) had to collaborate in one accessible product under real-time data constraints.

Process
  • UX audit against Nielsen's 10 heuristics; accessibility practice updated to the latest WCAG 3.0 standards.
  • Role-specific workflows and information architecture; full keyboard navigation; accessibility testing throughout.
  • Scenario-based user testing: four flows tested with recruited users, findings folded back into the UI iteratively.
