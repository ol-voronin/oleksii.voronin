About

A design leader who builds teams, strategy, and products in equal measure.

Over more than twelve years, I've moved fluidly between the drawing board and the leadership table — shaping enterprise platforms, AI-powered solutions, and customer-facing products for some of the world's most recognized organizations. My work sits where business strategy, user needs, and technical reality meet, and my job is to make that intersection feel effortless for the people who use what we build.

Today, as an Experience Design Manager at EPAM Systems, I lead UX strategy across global engagements while directly managing, mentoring, and growing a team of six designers spanning Middle to Lead levels. I care as much about the careers of the people I lead as I do about the experiences we ship — setting design direction, planning capacity, running reviews, and creating the conditions for designers to do their best work.

I've led discovery and strategy for healthcare ecosystems with Johnson & Johnson, Janssen, and Sandoz; defined immersive, AI-driven experiences showcased at Google Cloud Next; modernized data-heavy operational platforms for energy leaders like Halliburton, Schlumberger, and Kongsberg; and designed financial and marketplace products for Paxful, AWS/Omantel, and Interswitch. Across all of it, the constant is a human-centered approach grounded in research, design thinking, and measurable outcomes.

I'm equally comfortable facilitating an executive pre-sales workshop, defining an information architecture for a regulated multi-market platform, or pairing with engineers to protect design quality in delivery. Whatever the engagement, my aim is the same: clarity, usability, and real business value.
